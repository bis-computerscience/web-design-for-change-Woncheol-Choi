Vocabulary List

Word Definition
Birth Rate The ratio between the number of liveborn births in a year and the average total population of that year.
Liveborn A baby that is born alive.
Average Total Population The average number of people living in a specific area during a certain period.
Fertility Rate The average number of children a woman is expected to give birth to during her lifetime.
Projections Predictions or estimates of future events based on current data or trends.
Maintain To keep in an existing state or condition.
Economic Prosperity A state of economic growth and good fortune.
Dynamism The quality of being characterized by vigorous activity and progress.
Workforce The group of people who are employed or available for employment.
Consumption The use of goods and services by households.
National Security The safety and defense of a nation.
Provocations Actions or speech that make someone angry or prompt a response.
Housing Costs The expense associated with owning or renting a home.
Competitive Environment A situation where individuals or organizations compete against each other.
Gender Tensions Strain or conflict arising from differences in gender roles and expectations.
Monthly Allowances Regular financial payments given each month.
